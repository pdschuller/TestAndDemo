Adrian Adioetomo

by Phil Schuller

Adrian plays resonator guitar with a slide. This means that he can slide up and down the neck of the guitar, and mix in crazy licks with his free fingers, Fasten your seatbelts,

Part of Adrian is dark. I think he understands the truth of what Robert Johnson and Son House et al were singing about maybe better than anyone. Another part of Adrian is a very lighted hearted beautiul Dude. This is why we love him.

At a Robert Johnson festival in the namesakes hometown a few years back. Adrian stepped up on an open mic stage and was tipped $300 by the appreciative and astonished crowd.

In Indonsia he won the equivaliant of a Grammy for his EP. You can look it up.

So you gotta come out n see us - I'm just garnish. Adrian is the real deal. During a future Garammys show you will say, Oh wow, I saw that dude at the 565 Club back in 2024!

His contemporary-americana music takes the delta-blues to a new level.

He came up through an Americn sponsored high school in Indonesia and arrived in the US 2 year ago. ,

He’s been playing guitar for 10 years, and has been performing in Indonesia for 5 years. He’s played in many different venues, from small cafes to large festivals. He’s also been featured on TV and radio.

Here in Pittsburgh, he’s looking to start his new journey in his musical career.
