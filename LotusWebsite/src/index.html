The Surface Tension Of Water.


My Father discovered this process when I was about 14. I have always remembered it and until a few weeks ago, I just figured he was misataken.

Remember? We talked about the surface tension of water at Butter Milk Falls when we we're looking at water spiders "walking" upon the surface of the water. - where those kids were jumpimg off the cliff - YIIIKES

The Experiment:

  1. Pretty much fill a glass w water and put a sopping wet wash cloth in it, exactly like the picture above.
  2. The glass should be in a sink or the wash tub.
  3. Notice how far the water is from the top of your glass, and make a mental note.
  4. Go away for a half hour or so.
  5. Come back and observe how far the water is from the top of the glass. You will rely on the mental note you made in step 3.
  • What happened?
  • Hint: remember "The Surface Tension Of Water."
  • Did water flow straight up?! - defying the laws of gravity?
  • Ha ha. My Dad knew what he had observed.
